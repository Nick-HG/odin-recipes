Shirodashi Ramen

Description

This recipe is to make traditional ramen, a classic japanese dish which contains a broth often made from bone, a meat, an egg, noodles and a choice of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • bone broth
  • 1 egg
  • pork
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. Let the bone broth sit over night in the water to marinade
  2. Place the pork meat in the oven at 200c for 30 minutes or until tender
  3. Boil the egg in a pan of boiling water until the yolk is half cooked and then place in a bowl of cold water
  4. Chop your vegetables of choice and prepare to cook
  5. Cook the noodles in a pan of boiling water until they are soft
  6. Combine all cooked ingredients in a bowl and serve
